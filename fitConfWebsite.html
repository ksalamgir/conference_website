About the Conference

FIT is a long-running international conference focusing on recent advances in computer science, engineering, and related fields. FIT provides a forum for researchers, practitioners, and students to share ideas, network, and explore future research directions.

Topics include—but are not limited to—NLP & Generative AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Science, Computer Vision, Cyber Security, Cloud & HPC, Networks, Remote Sensing, and Water Informatics.

Call for Papers

Authors are invited to submit original, previously unpublished papers (up to 6 pages) in IEEE double-column format. Submissions should be in PDF and will be handled electronically through the conference submission system.
